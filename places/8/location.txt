National War Museum in Pune is a war memorial dedicated to post-Independence war martyrs. It was built in 1997 by the citizens of Pune who formed a war memorial fund to keep it financially stable. It was inaugurated and opened to the public on August 15, 1998, at Ghorpadi.National War Museum is located at Pune Cantonment, opposite Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Stadium on Veer Santaji Ghorpade Road. It is about 3 km away from Pune Railway Station and about 12 km away from Pune Airport. It is easily accessible from Pune by various modes of transport such as train, bus, cab or car. You can also visit other places of interest nearby such as Aga Khan Palace, Sinhagad Fort and Darshan Museum.
