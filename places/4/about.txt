The Empress Garden located in Pune was earlier known as "Soldier Garden". This garden was created in the year 1838. This popular garden is a dream-like place to visit in Pune city and is maintained by the Agro-Horticulture Society of Western India. The Empress Garden is well maintained and the main objective of this society is to protect the unusual flora of the area. There are rare varieties of flowers and trees in this garden.Many flower shows are organized here by the Agro-Horticulture Society so that people get to know about the unique species of flora. A stream flows around the garden, which is an added attraction here. This garden is amazingly natural and it attracts all the tourists visiting it.
