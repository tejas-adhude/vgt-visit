The temple is well-connected by road to other parts of the city and the state. You can drive by car, bike or scooter to the temple and park near the entrance. You can also take a bus, a cab or an auto rickshaw to the temple from any part of the city.
The nearest railway station is Pune Junction, which is about 4 km away from the temple. You can take a cab, an auto rickshaw or a bus from the station to the temple.
The nearest airport is Pune International Airport, which is about 12 km away from the temple. You can take a cab, an auto rickshaw or a bus from the airport to the temple.
