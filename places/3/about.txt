Mata Vaishno Devi Temple is located in the heart of the city of Pimpri-Chinchwad in Maharashtra. It is a replica of the original Mata Vaishno Devi Shrine located in Katra, Jammu. The temple is accessible to people of all walks of life and has the same structure as the one in Katra. The temple has a cave that devotees have to pass through before they can take the blessing of Devi1. The entrance is crossing by a built mountain, and next, you have to enter a cave and walk through water. You need to bend and walk through the water to reach the main temple
