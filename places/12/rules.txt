You need to buy a ticket to enter the caves. The ticket booth is at the entrance at the top of the hill. The entry fee is 25 rupees for Indians and 300 rupees for foreigners.You should respect the sanctity of the caves and avoid making noise or littering inside them.You should not touch or damage any of the sculptures or carvings in the caves.You should not take photographs or videos inside the caves without permission from the authorities.You should dress modestly and cover your head and shoulders when entering the caves, especially if you want to visit the Hindu temple next to them.You should be careful while climbing the steps to reach the caves, as they can be slippery or steep. You should also avoid visiting during heavy rains or landslides.
