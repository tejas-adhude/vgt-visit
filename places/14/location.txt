Bhima River originates near Bhimashankar Temple in the Bhimashankar hills in Khed Taluka on the western side of the Western Ghats, known as Sahyadri, in Pune District, Maharashtra state¹². The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas (self-manifested lingams) in the world. The temple is also a key pilgrimage centre and a wildlife sanctuary.
